# Alchemist's Automatons

> An idle automation RPG where you play as an alchemist who crafts golems to gather resources from the world — while you brew potions and upgrade your workshop.

**Play it live:** [GitHub Pages](https://victorinno.github.io/alch_auto/)

---

## Gameplay

- **Craft Golems** — Build Clay, Iron, Crystal, and Moon Golems using gathered resources.
- **Send Golems to Zones** — Dispatch your automatons to forests, mines, swamps, ruins, and volcanoes.
- **Alchemy Lab** — Brew potions and elixirs to earn Gold and Arcane Essence.
- **Upgrade Workshop** — Unlock more golem slots, new recipes, and powerful upgrades.
- **Offline Progress** — Your golems keep working while you're away!

## Zones

| Zone | Resources | Danger |
|---|---|---|
| Whispering Forest | Herbs, Crystals | Safe |
| Iron Depths | Iron, Crystals | Low |
| Sulfur Swamp | Sulfur, Herbs | Low |
| Ancient Ruins | Moonstone, Essence | Medium |
| Ember Volcano | Sulfur, Moonstone | High |

## Tech

Pure HTML + CSS + JavaScript. No dependencies. Runs entirely in the browser.
Save data stored in `localStorage`.
